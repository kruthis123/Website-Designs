My hobbies


Singing

🎶

I enjoy listening to music and singing bollywood songs. I mostly listen to slow and soft music while I also enjoy fast numbers. I've not been trained professionally for singing but I do believe and have heard from others that I have a sweet voice.


Dancing

💃

Dancing is my most favourite hobby and makes me very happy and also relieves my stress. I've been learning different forms of dance from when I was a kid. I've majorly been training in Kathak, a classical dance form of North India, for about 7 years now and continuing at Abhinava Dance Company, Bangalore.
